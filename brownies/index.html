Cooking Baking with Chris

Hello, Brownies

I decided that I would be testing out some recipes from my new cookbook from The King Arthur Flour Company. I told my coworker Bear that I would bring him some brownies this weekend, so I set out on my mission to produce said brownies. This is the first time I've made brownies without a boxed mix. I don't think I'll ever go back to using boxed mixes for my baked goods. Not when I have fantastic recipes such as the one I followed here.

First things first, we'll be needing an ingredients list:

  • 6 ounces unsweetened baking chocolate
  • 12 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 5 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/3 cup light corn syrup (optional)
  • 1 1/3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped walnuts
